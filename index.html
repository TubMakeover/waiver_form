The fumes from the refinishing process typically dissipate within 3 to 6 hours after the work is completed. If you return home and notice a strong odor that causes discomfort, we recommend returning later or keeping doors and windows open to ventilate the area. Sensitivity to these fumes may vary from person to person depending on factors such as age, medical history, and others.

It is not safe for pets to be in the area during the refinishing process, as the fumes can be too strong for them, potentially causing respiratory, eye, and stomach irritation. Even after the work is completed, it is advisable to keep pets away until the odor significantly diminishes. The use of fans can help speed up ventilation.
